StarChat

Welcome! StarChat lets you roleplay as anybody you want. You will then be paired with someone for a spontaneous, improv chat adventure.

You can be a 'dragon slayer', a 'magical penguin' etc. You might even be an 'empathetic listener' and provide free online therapy. The choice is yours...

To begin, simply enter your fantasy character and click 'Start a Chat'.

Note: Kids and innocents use our site, so let's keep it clean, fun, and family-friendly.
